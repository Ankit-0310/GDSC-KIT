About Us

Concept of GDSC

The GDSC program serves as a grassroots platform through which Google offers students essential development, mobile, and web development skills, with the overarching goal of enhancing their employability prospects.

Why GDSC?

To nurture students development skills, empower them to solve problems using technology, and inspire them to become exceptional developers and change-makers on a global scale.

Target audience

GDSC activities are targeted at University students and any others including faculty members who want to learn development skills & work to solve real-life problems.
